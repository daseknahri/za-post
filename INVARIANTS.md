# INVARIANTS — the anti-regression contract

This file is the consolidated **"never weaken these"** reference for za-post. Every rule below encodes a failure this app has already suffered or is one wrong edit away from suffering. The two ways this app can hurt an operator's Facebook accounts are **publishing the same post twice** (a visible duplicate that gets accounts flagged) and **posting the same comment/link twice** (the top ban trigger). The invariants that guard those two outcomes are the **crown jewels** and are marked `sacred`.

Read this before touching any anchored line. An anchor is `file:line` at the time of writing — if the line has drifted, find the described logic, don't trust the number blindly.

Severity ladder: **Sacred** (a duplicate post/comment or destroyed account data — irreversible harm on real FB accounts) → **High** (data loss, ban-risk, security/licensing bypass, wedged runs) → **Medium** (silent corruption of counters/cookies/reports).

---

## SACRED — the double-post / double-comment traps (crown jewels)

These six are the reason the app is trustworthy at scale. A regression here does not throw an error — it silently duplicates content on live accounts or wipes the operator's dealt-state. **Nothing here may be weakened, "simplified," or made "more aggressive on retry" without adversarial multi-agent verification.**

| ID | Rule | WHY it exists (what breaks) | Anchor |
|----|------|------------------------------|--------|
| **INV-01** | Once the Post-button click actually fires (`publishClicked` is set **only after** `clickPostButton` returns true), that `(post, group)` is **NEVER** retried within the group loop or the outer catch. A throw *before* the click still allows a safe retry. | Re-running a group after the click publishes the post a **second time** — a visible duplicate. This is the core double-post trap; pre-publish transient retries are gated on `!publishClicked`. | `automation/worker.js:2728`, `automation/worker.js:3106` |
| **INV-02** | On a `stopped`- or `timeout`-but-landed publish, the post is **COUNTED** (`posted++` / `markDelivered`) rather than left un-dealt. The timeout path re-confirms **READ-ONLY** (feed rescan / dialog-closed) and **NEVER** re-clicks Post; `waitForPublish` never shortens its timeout ceiling. | An un-dealt post that actually published gets re-posted next Start — a duplicate. Missing one post on Stop is strictly better than duplicating one; a too-short ceiling false-timeouts → re-posts → duplicates. | `automation/worker.js:2737`, `automation/worker.js:817` |
| **INV-03** | In `addFirstComment`, once the submitting Enter is pressed, `submitted=true` and the function **never** returns the retryable `'failed'` (post-Enter issues return `'unconfirmed'`/`'not_visible'`). The comment retry loop (inline and Phase-2) retries **ONLY** on `cres==='failed'`. | The caller retries on `'failed'`; returning `'failed'` after Enter re-runs the flow and posts a **DUPLICATE comment**. This is the core double-comment trap. | `automation/worker.js:1010`, `automation/worker.js:3042` |
| **INV-04** | Only **NON-landed** comment outcomes are pushed to `commentQueue` for reserve rescue. The `*_landed` variants (`blocked_account_landed` / `blocked_comment_landed`) mark the task **DONE** and are never re-queued; rescue treats a landed-then-blocked result as done. | Queuing a landed comment makes a reserve re-place it — a **double-comment**. The `_landed` variants exist precisely so a comment that landed just before FB walled the account is not re-queued. | `automation/worker.js:3060`, `automation/rescue.js:98` |
| **INV-05** | A published post's dealt-state (shared `_dealt` set **OR** per-agent rotation pointer) must be persisted to disk **BEFORE** the run continues. If the write fails the run **HALTS** (`_stop=true`) rather than proceed, and the pointer is **never** rolled back after advancing — even on a persist failure. | A post published but not recorded as dealt is re-dealt and **RE-POSTED** after a crash/restart (cross-run double-post); a rollback makes the **same** run re-pick and re-post it (same-run double-post). | `automation/orchestrator.js:1193`, `automation/orchestrator.js:1808` |
| **INV-06** | `this._owed` must be reconciled and written in **lock-step** (same `saveRotation`) with the pointer advance it depends on, via the inline `_reconcileOwedFor` call. The per-`(post,group)` delivery ledger key **MUST** be scoped by `_dkScope` (per-agent for Daily Rotation, fleet-wide otherwise) consistently across `markDelivered` / `alreadyDelivered` and every owed filter. | A hard kill between pointer-advance and owed-reconcile, or a scope mismatch, either **re-owes a delivered group** (double-post) or **drops an un-reached one** (silent skip). | `automation/orchestrator.js:1804`, `automation/orchestrator.js:2855` |

### The other sacred rules — landing on the *right* post, approving the *right* card

At 400-account scale, other accounts post the **same campaign caption to the same group**. These invariants stop the app from ever guessing which post is "ours."

| ID | Rule | WHY it exists (what breaks) | Anchor |
|----|------|------------------------------|--------|
| **INV-07** | A comment lands only after an **FB-post-id match** (real `/^\d{8,}$/` id, not a local library id), a **SINGLE unambiguous caption match**, or (multiple same-caption posts) the article **AUTHORED BY US**. Otherwise it **REFUSES** (`skipped`) and never guesses. | At scale another account posts the same caption to the same group; a blind topmost-match lands the link on a **stranger's or another account's post**. | `automation/worker.js:1193` |
| **INV-08** | Moderator approval is strictly **fail-closed**: click a card only if its caption matches a **this-cycle held snippet (≥12 chars)** **AND** its author is one of **OUR `fbDisplayName`** (or unreadable) **AND** it has an **approve** (not decline) button. Reject a button whose caption-bearing ancestor holds **≥2 approve buttons**; success counts only on **tagged-button detachment**. | The admin account must never approve a **stranger's held spam** or double-approve. Weakening any conjunct approves the wrong post; a short snippet substring-matches a stranger's pending post. | `automation/moderator.js:250`, `automation/moderator.js:216` |
| **INV-09** | Never re-post a held post when the original is already public: `isContentLive` must return `'live'` (→ `runRepost` returns `{alreadyLive:true}`) whenever OUR post is confirmed public via permalink id/caption or **author-aware** feed match; a **readable stranger's** same-caption post is **NOT** `'live'`. | Re-posting an FB-auto-released held post creates a **public duplicate**; a false `'live'` from a stranger's caption strands our held post and re-homes its comment onto the wrong post. | `automation/repost.js:151`, `automation/repost.js:122` |

## HIGH — data loss, ban-risk, security, licensing, wedged runs

| ID | Rule | WHY it exists (what breaks) | Anchor |
|----|------|------------------------------|--------|
| **INV-10** | Every renderer-reachable IPC channel must appear in `preload.js` `ALLOWED_CHANNELS`; the generic `invoke()` rejects any channel not in the Set; the main window keeps `contextIsolation:true` / `nodeIntegration:false` with the preload bridge as the only exposure. | The allowlist is the **only** barrier stopping injected/XSS renderer JS from calling sensitive handlers like `get-account-credentials` (decrypts FB passwords) or `batch-account-action` (bulk-delete + profile wipe). | `preload.js:6`, `preload.js:26`, `main.js:483` |
| **INV-11** | A cookie jar may be written (`writeCookies`, a **full overwrite**) only when it is a **LIVE session** — `c_user` **AND** non-empty `xs` present. Chrome-import accounts match **STRICTLY by `c_user`** (a name collision with a different `c_user` creates a new account, never overwrites). | Persisting a login-wall/soft-logout jar destroys the account's last-good recovery jar, stranding it `needs_login`; a `c_user`-blind match hijacks an unrelated account and overwrites its session. | `main.js:1525`, `main.js:407` |
| **INV-12** | All data mutations (renderer, remote API, status writes, orchestrator) go through the serialized `store.update` / `updateModeration` / `updateComments` write-chains — never a bare save on a stale snapshot. Every persisted file is written via `writeFileAtomic` (tmp → looped `writeSync` → `fsync` → rename). | Concurrent writers lost-update each other (silently losing held posts / orphaned comments); without `fsync`, a crash after rename leaves the **0-byte file this app already suffered**. | `lib/store.js:293`, `lib/store.js:207` |
| **INV-13** | When `load()` flags `_primaryUnreadable` (existing `data.json` read failed, bytes intact), `update()` must **SKIP `save()` entirely**. Only a **successful-read-but-failed-parse** may quarantine the primary as `.corrupt-<ts>`. | Persisting a `blank()`/`.bak` result over a good-but-transiently-locked primary (Defender/OneDrive/indexer) **wipes the operator's real accounts/posts/groups**; the original code conflated a transient lock with corruption. | `lib/store.js:303`, `lib/store.js:256` |
| **INV-14** | The full-blob `save-data` handler must take **RUNTIME/bridge-owned** account fields (`status`, `rateLimitedUntil`, `daily`, `chrome*`) from the **live disk copy** and union back any disk-only account/group/post missing from the renderer snapshot. Deletion goes only through dedicated delete handlers. | The renderer snapshot is stale; overwriting runtime fields resets a live rate-limit cooldown or daily count (**deepening FB bans**), and dropping disk-only records erases accounts the Chrome bridge created concurrently. | `main.js:735` |
| **INV-15** | `setAccountStatus` must not downgrade an active rate-limit/checkpoint/verification/disabled flag set by the run (`preserveAttentionStatus`); `c_user` is the **PRIMARY** auth check, DOM only for picker/checkpoint detection. | A headless cookie probe reads `logged_in` even for an account blocked from **POSTING**; clearing the flag lets the orchestrator re-select a blocked account. | `main.js:1367`, `lib/store.js:567` |
| **INV-16** | `overLimit` is the authoritative account/group creation gate on **EVERY** create path (`create-account`, `add-accounts-bulk`, `add-group`, `add-groups-bulk`); posting/login entry points reachable via the remote API (`onStart`, `loginAccount`) **AND** the UI (`start-automation`) must each independently refuse when `_licenseState.enforced && !_licenseState.valid`. | A direct IPC or remote HTTP call bypasses UI guards; the remote server/bridge run **before** the UI license gate. (The Chrome-bridge create path deliberately omits `overLimit` because every tier is `Infinity`.) | `main.js:1858`, `main.js:575` |
| **INV-17** | All browsers **MUST** launch via `launchStealth` (real Chrome, `ignoreDefaultArgs:['--enable-automation']`, `BASE_ARGS` merged **after** the caller spread); the stealth `user-agent-override` evasion **MUST stay disabled**; `applyProxyGeo` must never **INVENT** a timezone/locale. | These are the load-bearing anti-bot guarantees; the UA-override evasion forges a 2022-era headless fingerprint FB flags with an **endless captcha**, and a fabricated geo mismatches the proxy IP. | `lib/browser.js:70`, `lib/browser.js:22` |
| **INV-18** | The anti-spam comment floor is **NEVER 0** and the group floor is preserved; every human gap is a **random draw** that never shortens below its safe floor (group ~20s / comment ~30s normal; INSTANT mode drops the comment floor to a hard 4s minimum (Facebook's post→link danger threshold); turbo ~8s/12s). | Instant post→link is Facebook's top ban trigger and can fire before the post is permalink-resolvable; a **predictable constant** delay is itself a bot signature. | `automation/worker.js:103` |
| **INV-19** | Never publish an image-less/caption-less post: a missing file input, unconfirmed image preview (`<N`), or an empty editor when caption+image were intended throws a `'transient:'` error (routes to **pre-publish retry**), never a Post click. Caption entry always focuses+clears first; a present-but-unverified caption is **ACCEPTED** rather than re-entered. | An attached image alone enables FB's Post button, so an empty caption would publish **image-only** and falsely count as success (silent content loss); re-entering an already-present caption is what historically **DOUBLED** it. | `automation/worker.js:2661`, `automation/worker.js:2544` |
| **INV-20** | An account with **NO assigned groups** is never assigned a post and must never fall back to posting to **every** group; a `notfound` (published but never public) post routes to `heldRecords` for **MODERATOR approval**, NOT the reserve comment-rescue queue. | A group-less fallback spams **ALL** groups from unconfigured/stand-in accounts; a held post is not public, so no reserve can comment on it — only moderator approval makes it public. | `automation/worker.js:1817`, `automation/worker.js:3076` |
| **INV-21** | Both concurrency-pool drain loops (main and reserve-takeover) **MUST break** when `launchNext()` can't place a queued account (all proxy IPs busy) — never busy-spin. The run-active flag is written **durably** (temp → `fsync` → rename), cleared on every clean-exit path, left set **only** on a hard kill. | A synchronous busy-wait pegs 100% CPU and **wedges the run forever** (no promise/Stop/Finish resolves); a torn run-flag write suppresses crash-resume while a stale-set flag resurrects a finished run. | `automation/orchestrator.js:1950`, `main.js:199`, `main.js:259` |
| **INV-22** | The remote API token comparison stays **constant-time** with a length guard (Buffer both sides, equal-length, `crypto.timingSafeEqual`); when a token is set it gates **BOTH** `/api` and the `/uploads`+`/images` static dirs; the rate limiter is mounted **BEFORE** the token gate; remote responses never contain raw error text. | `===` opens a timing side-channel on the token; gating only `/api` lets anyone with the tunnel URL scrape client images; auth-before-limiter lets failed-auth floods bypass the throttle; raw errors leak filesystem/userData paths. | `server.js:103`, `server.js:107` |
| **INV-23** | license `writeCache` must be **atomic** (write `.tmp` then rename); `lastValidated` advances **ONLY** on a real server validation (offline preserves prev); device-lock fails **CLOSED** on a missing/mismatched hwid; the owner key is always valid; `readCache` distinguishes `ENOENT` (not activated) from an existing-but-unreadable file (retry, never valid); the ~6h re-check returns early on a transient lock. | A partial `license.json` forces an offline-impossible re-activation; a self-bumping `lastValidated` perpetually **self-extends the 7-day grace**, defeating revocation; accepting an absent hwid runs one key on many machines; treating a transient lock as invalid kills an overnight run. | `lib/license.js:51`, `lib/license.js:121`, `lib/license.js:139` |
| **INV-24** | Migration is **one-shot and marker-guarded**: `migrateLegacyUserDataOnce` must never seed over an install that already has `data.json` (writes the marker and returns), and the marker is written in **every** terminal branch. | Seeding stale legacy data over the operator's real install clobbers accounts/groups/posts/encrypted cookies; re-running overwrites freshly created data. | `main.js:59`, `main.js:50` |
| **INV-27** | Only the instance holding the **single-instance lock** may run boot side effects (`killOrphanChromium`, server start, auto-resume); `whenReady` early-returns when `!gotLock`. | A second instance kills the **first** instance's live run/login browsers (all under `ACCOUNTS_DIR`), starts a colliding server, and may double-resume. | `main.js:496` |
| **INV-28** | Prefetch-tab adopt **MUST rebind** `cdpSession` (and `hiddenWindowId`) to the adopted tab **before** closing the old tab. | The prefetch tab has its own CDP session; closing the original detaches the old `cdpSession`, silently breaking the CDP caption-paste and periodic off-screen re-park (which target a **session**, not a page) for the rest of the run. | `automation/worker.js:2385` |

## MEDIUM — silent corruption of counters, cookies, reports

| ID | Rule | WHY it exists (what breaks) | Anchor |
|----|------|------------------------------|--------|
| **INV-25** | `normalizeCookie` must force `secure:true` whenever `sameSite` resolves to `'None'`; the daily-cap window rolls over only on a **FORWARD** local-day move (`key > stored date`). | Chrome rejects a `SameSite=None` cookie without `secure:true`, and the one-by-one fallback then silently drops it (e.g. `xs`), **logging the account out**; a non-monotonic rollover lets a backward clock clear the count and bypass the cap. | `lib/store.js:445`, `lib/store.js:482` |
| **INV-26** | CSV audit fields starting with `=` `+` `-` `@` (or tab/CR) must be prefixed with a lone quote before escaping; `recordProgress` de-dupes a re-reported cell (decrement old status before incrementing new) and is the **single in-process writer** of the progress ledger. | Captions/comments/group names are FB-controlled text that would execute as spreadsheet formulas (**CSV injection**) when the operator opens `run-report.csv`; blind counting double-counts and makes the dashboard lie. | `lib/store.js:657`, `lib/store.js:697` |
